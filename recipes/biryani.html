Biryani

Description

It's an authentic south indian recipe consists of spices,rice and meat

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • spices
  • Rice
  • water
  • saffron
  • ghee

Steps

  1. Marinate chicken with curd, lemon, paprika and all spice powders.
  2. wash the rice and keep it aside.
  3. heat a pan and add oil.
  4. add chicken and let it cook until meat is cooked upto 80%.
  5. Other side, boil the rice, spices in the water and let it boil till the rice is cooked 80%.
  6. Now in another pan, add rice and add chicken on top off it.
  7. sprinkle a little bit ghee and saffron water and cook it on low flame for 10 min.
  8. make sure that the vapour doesn't evaporates out of it. that's called dum.
  9. serve it with raitha.
